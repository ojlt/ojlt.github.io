A list, in no particular order, of music I like. While you're here, do check out my [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@owainthorp3536) for the occasional upload.

## Johann Sebastian Bach

- **Keyboard:** The Well-Tempered Clavier (BWV 846–893)
- **Orchestral:** Brandenburg Concertos (BWV 1046–1051), Violin Concertos (BWV 1041–1043, incl. the Concerto for Two Violins)
- **Solo violin:** Sonatas and Partitas (BWV 1001–1006)
- **Choral:** Mass in B minor (BWV 232), St Matthew Passion (BWV 244)

## Béla Bartók

- **String Quartets:** No. 2 (Sz. 67), 4 (Sz. 91), 5 (Sz. 102), 6 (Sz. 114)
- **Orchestral:** Concerto for Orchestra (Sz. 116), Music for Strings, Percussion and Celesta (Sz. 106)
- **Piano Concerto:** No. 3 (Sz. 119)

- **Piano Sonatas:** No. 17 *Tempest* (Op. 31/2), 21 *Waldstein* (Op. 53), 23 *Appassionata* (Op. 57), 28 (Op. 101), 29 *Hammerklavier* (Op. 106), 31 (Op. 110), 32 (Op. 111)
